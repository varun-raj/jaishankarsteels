About us

We have employed experienced professionals with whose innovations we are able to customize the specific requirement of the clients. The latest machines have been installed to carry out the quality production in accordance to specifications desired by the clients. All the parameters that are closely monitored are the sizes of products, shapes, specifications, color combinations to be given to the final product and as well the quality of raw material that are to be used so as to bring out the final products in line with customization.The products offered by the firm are a class apart owing to its quality, workmanship and fine finish. For maintenance of regular quality of the products the firm is very particular in procuring the raw materials and fittings from reputed vendors only. There is no compromise on the quality whatsoever. The firm has employed technical and experienced professionals to take of various stages of production, quality control, processing, finishing, packing and delivery etc. The proprietor of the firm takes active interest in all the activities and is very particular about the quality of the goods and satisfaction of the clients. The display of finished products is done neatly and attractively in a large hall which allows the clients to walk in and inspect the goods closely for the accuracy of defined parameters. The activity of the firm has been divided into various working sections like that for storage and production and finishing. Administrative Block comprising of Proprietor’s Room, Sales & Marketing and Accounts section. The firm is also known to best to follow ethical business policy of fair trade practice which has accounted for the ever growing goodwill of the firm. The firm is also very particular in honoring its commitment of delivery schedules to keep the client’s satisfaction intact.
